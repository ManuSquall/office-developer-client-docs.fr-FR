### <a name="to-flush-all-incoming-or-outgoing-queues-in-a-single-operation"></a><span data-ttu-id="e64c9-110">Pour vider toutes les files d’attente entrantes ou sortantes en une seule opération</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e64c9-110">To flush all incoming or outgoing queues in a single operation</span></span>
